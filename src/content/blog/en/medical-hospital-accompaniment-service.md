## Real-Life Scenarios

### When an elderly patient needs to go to hospital but nobody from his family is free to accompany him.

Our agent(s) will come pick up the patient at his residence and will bring him to his medical appointment. Our staff will stay with the patient while waiting to the doctor, facilitating and handling any additional paperwork needed. If needed, we can also provide translation services to ensure the patient receives accurate advice and consultation if he does not speak Thai or English. After the consultation, our staff will assist the patient with any additional paperwork, scheduling follow-up appointments or purchasing prescribed medications. Our agent(s) will then safely accompany the patient back to his residence.

### A child has a medical appointment but the child's parents are busy at work all day and they can't leave their work to bring their child at the appointment.

Our agency can send an agent to bring their child to his appointment, making sure that he is not alone throughout the day, bring back the child to his parents and can report to the parents the summary of the appointment.
