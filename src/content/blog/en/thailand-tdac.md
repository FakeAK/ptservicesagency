Thailand has officially launched the Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC), replacing the traditional paper-based TM6 form for all foreign nationals entering the country by air, land, or sea. Effective from May 1, 2025, this mandatory digital system is part of Thailand's initiative to modernize its immigration procedures, enhance border security, and streamline the entry process for travelers. Learn in this article what is it, when and how to submit it.

***

## What is the Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC)?

The Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC) is an online system that replaces the traditional paper arrival card (also known as TM6). It is designed to facilitate faster and more secure entry into Thailand, while also enhancing public health management. The TDAC applies to all travelers entering the country by land, air, or sea. The TDAC collects essential information such as personal details, passport data, travel itinerary, accommodation address in Thailand, and a basic health declaration. This data is integrated with Thailand's biometric database, allowing for more efficient security screening at immigration checkpoints.

## Who is required to submit the TDAC?

All tourists and foreign nationals entering Thailand are required to complete the Thailand Digital Arrival Card (TDAC) prior to their arrival.

## When should the information be submitted?

Travelers are required to complete the TDAC within 72 hours prior to their arrival in Thailand. While assistance will be available at border checkpoints for those unable to submit the form ahead of time, it's strongly recommended to complete it before departure to avoid potential delays.

## How to fill the TDAC?

Visit the following website: [https://tdac.immigration.go.th](https://tdac.immigration.go.th)

**Select Submission Type:**
- Individual Submission
- Group Submission (up to 10 persons per submission)

**Prepare the Required Information:**
- Passport details
- Personal information
- Travel information
- Accommodation details in Thailand
- Health information

**How to Complete and Submit the Form:**
- Click the "Arrival Card" button.
- Fill in the required information step-by-step, or upload an image of the MRZ (Machine Readable Zone of the passport) to autofill details.
- Enter accommodation and travel plans.
- Provide health information if additionally required (applicable for travelers arriving from high-risk countries).
- Review all information carefully before submitting.
- Provide an email address to receive the Thailand Digital Arrival Card document.
- Download or print a copy to present during your stay in Thailand.

**Note:** the TDAC can be submitted individually or as a group (up to 10 persons per submission).

## How to update TDAC information if needed?

1. Visit the website: [https://tdac.immigration.go.th](https://tdac.immigration.go.th)
2. Select "Update Arrival Card"
3. Enter the required search information, such as the TDAC number, passport number, date of birth, and nationality
4. Update the necessary information and confirm the submission

**Note:** certain information cannot be modified, including name, surname, passport number, nationality, and date of birth.

## Additional Informations

- All information must be entered in English only.
- The TDAC is not a visa! Travelers must apply for a visa separately if required.
- It is strongly recommended to verify all information carefully before submission.
- The system can autofill information from the MRZ (Machine Readable Zone) of the passport, but travelers must review and confirm its accuracy.
- Health information must be additionally provided if the traveler is arriving from a country subject to special health requirements designated by the Ministry of Public Health.
